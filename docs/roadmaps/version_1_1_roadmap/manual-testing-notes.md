# Notes while playing 2026_03_13

- during upgrades, the sound of the car engine is held, causing annoyance, clear game sound 
- FPS Drop after unpausing / muting sound: ~12FPS - 200ms (made FPS issue worse)
- FPS Drop during upgrade -> new wave, FPS drop ~22 FPS - 150ms
- Trail Burn is disgustingly overpowered, can almost make a wall that they all die in. 
- Known interesting behavior: if you spam the drift button you can gain abnormal speeds, out running basically everything if done right
    - Afterburn and Drift king make this better
- Known issue/behavior: the mud collision reduce the players accelerations for a little too long, really rough recovery
- Near misses, drift, and damage tick text messages on top of the player is obscuring the vision of the gameplay a little too much, might feel nicer to have a sort of log of events scrolling as they occur on either side of the screen, sort of like a chat
